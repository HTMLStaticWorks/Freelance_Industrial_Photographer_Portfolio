How I Deliver

The Way I Shoot

My systematic, 6-step project lifecycle ensures absolute safety compliance, site efficiency, and premium image delivery.

1

Discovery & Safety Review

I discuss your engineering project scope, site boundaries, schedule timeline, and specific hazard credentials (OSHA, TWIC, site briefings).

2

Shot List & Site Scouting

I scout machinery angles, ambient light windows, grid lines, and shifts to map a zero-downtime photography schedule.

3

Site Briefing & PPE Check

On shoot day, I attend the local site safety induction, sync coordinate channels with the site manager, and wear certified PPE.

4

Ground & Drone Capture

I shoot ground-level details, automated machinery, and FAA drone surveys, maintaining absolute compliance alongside active machinery.

5

RAW Post-Processing

I transfer RAW camera profiles and calibrate colors, balancing metallic highlights, furnace glows, and shadow details into cinematic outputs.

6

Proposal Handover & Licensing

High-resolution digital deliveries, complete with print-ready media files and corporate licensing rights for marketing, PR, and bids.
